Custom widget to to display shoppable looks on your website.

== Description ==

Stylinity makes social media shoppable. We connect bloggers, influencers, brands, and consumers to help create 
great social content you can shop. Stylinity's suite of easy-to-use services help bloggers, influencers, and brands 
to create, share, track and analyze shoppable content across Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, & more. 
As a consumer, you'll be able to shop directly from your favourite influencers by using our 'Buy Now' buttons 
that are embedded in blogs and on your favorite social channels.

Use the Stylinity Widget to link your Stylinity account with your Wordpress website. Display your shoppable looks
directly on your blog. Customize its width, number of looks, color, etc. to make it look great on your blog.

== Installation ==

Option 1 (recommended):
1. Install Stylinity Widget through the Wordpress Plugins screen

Option 2:
1. Download Stylinity Widget through the Wordpress Plugins Directory
2. Upload the plugin files to the '/wp-content/plugins/plugin-name' directory where your website files are located

Activation:
1. Under 'Installed Plugins' in your Admin dashboard, locate Stylinity Widget and click 'Activate'
2. In 'Appearance' under 'Widgets,' drag our widget from 'Available Widgets' to the area of your website that you want it to appear.
3. Click on the widget, and fill in all the required fields with your preferred information (make sure "User ID" is your Stylinity account username)
4. Save your changes

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I center the title within the sidebar? =

Insert the following code into your custom.css theme file:

.sidebar .widget-title {
text-align: center;
}

= Can I add multiple Stylinity widgets? =

Our widget looks best on your website as a sidebar widget, but it's up to you!
